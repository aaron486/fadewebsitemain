01  "How Much Have You Won?"

Man on the Street
▾

Go out in public — bars, tailgates, campuses, sports events — and ask real people about their betting habits. Capture genuine, funny, unfiltered reactions.

Hooks

"I asked strangers how much they've won betting."

"We asked people at the bar their worst bet ever."

"How much have you actually won sports betting?"

Example Script
Script Flow
Creator approaches people at a sports bar / tailgate / campus.
Creator: "Hey — real quick — how much have you won sports betting?"
Cut between 3–5 reactions. Some honest, some funny, some delusional.
Person 1: "I'm up like $200... this year... okay maybe lifetime."
Person 2: "Bro I don't even wanna talk about it."
Person 3: "I hit a 10-leg parlay once. Been chasing that high ever since."
Creator: "If you actually tracked your bets you'd know. That's what Fade does."
CTA: "Download Fade — link in bio."
Ideas

Film at sports bars during games, college campuses on game day, tailgates, watch parties, or anywhere sports fans hang out. The more real and unscripted, the better.

02  "Fade of the Week"

Weekly Series
▾

A recurring weekly series where you highlight the worst bet, worst take, or biggest L of the week in sports betting. Think community roast meets weekly recap.

Hooks

"This is the Fade of the Week."

"Someone actually bet this... and lost."

"The worst bet of the week goes to..."

Example Script
Script Flow
Creator: "Welcome back to Fade of the Week."
"This week's winner... someone bet a 12-leg parlay on a Tuesday."
Show the bet / screenshot / recreation.
Creator: "Bro... 12 legs... on a Tuesday... what are we doing."
"If you're making bets like this, you need Fade."
"It actually tracks your bets so you can see exactly where you're going wrong."
CTA: "Download Fade — stop guessing, start tracking."
Ideas

Pull from real betting culture — bad parlays, overreactions, public money fails. You can feature your own bad bets, friends' bets, or viral betting Ls from the week. Make it a series people come back to.

Content Guidelines

Platforms

TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, X (Twitter)

Format

Vertical (9:16) · 15–45 seconds · Authentic / conversational

Do's

Show Fade in your content — the app, stats, leaderboards, or FadeIQ insights.

Be authentic. Content should feel real, not like a scripted ad.

Show real reactions. Film with friends. Include humor and real betting culture.

Tag us on every post:

Instagram: @fade.bet

X: @Fade_Bet_

TikTok: @fade_bet

Use #TeamFade, #FadeBet, and #FadeIQ in all posts.

Don'ts

Don't refer to Fade as a sports betting app — no bets are placed on Fade.

Don't make guaranteed income or winning claims.

Don't use other brand logos or competitor names.

Don't post without mentioning Fade in at least one shot.

Closing Lines

"Download the Fade app and bet together" · "Download Fade"

Creative Freedom

You're encouraged to bring your own ideas. Possible directions: man on the street interviews, sports bar content, betting with friends, reacting to FadeIQ insights, exposing betting mistakes, weekly series.

Possible opening: "I'm on #TeamFade."
