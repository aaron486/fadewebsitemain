Frequently Asked Questions

What is Fade?

Fade is a social sports betting platform that connects bettors, syncs sportsbook data, and provides AI-powered insights. Fade is not a sportsbook and does not facilitate real-money wagering.

How does FadeSync work?

FadeSync links to your existing sportsbook accounts (FanDuel, DraftKings, BetMGM, Caesars, ESPN Bet, Kalshi) and automatically imports your betting history. We never access your payment credentials.

Is my data safe?

Yes. We use industry-standard encryption and security measures. We never sell your personal information. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

What age do I need to be?

You must be at least 18 years old (or 21 where required by your jurisdiction) to use Fade.
