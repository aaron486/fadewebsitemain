FAQ

Common questions.

Is my money safe?

Yes. Fade Sync is read-only — it never deposits, withdraws, or places wagers. You trade with your own capital on your own accounts, and your data is anonymized in any published results.

How do I earn the $25?

Place at least 10 World Cup trades on Kalshi over the next two weeks. Track your progress in the app — payout details are shared once you're set up.

Why prediction markets?

We're testing whether everyday bettors do better trading prediction markets than at their sportsbook. Your sportsbook history is the baseline; your Kalshi trades are the comparison. Trade your own way — there are no right or wrong picks.
