The study

Bet the World Cup.
We track the truth.

Fade is studying whether everyday sports bettors actually perform better trading prediction markets than betting at their sportsbook. The World Cup is the perfect testing ground — massive volume, global markets, and everyone has an opinion.

You bet your way. We measure how you really did — grading every position against the closing line, not the highlight reel.

What counts

The ground rules.

What you do

  • Keep your sportsbooks + Kalshi synced for the whole window
  • Trade World Cup markets on Kalshi with your own capital
  • Stay active — hit the minimum trades for the tournament window
  • Bet your way — there are no right or wrong picks

Good to know

  • Read-only — Fade never places or settles bets for you
  • Your data is anonymized in any published results
  • Participation is voluntary — you can leave at any time
  • This is research, not betting or financial advice
