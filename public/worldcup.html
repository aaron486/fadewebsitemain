FAQ

Common questions.

Is my money safe?

Yes. Fade Sync is read-only — it can see your bets and outcomes to measure performance, but it never deposits, withdraws, or places wagers. You trade with your own capital on your own accounts.

How do I get the $25?

Complete the study — sync your books and trade at least 10 World Cup events on Kalshi across the tournament. Payout details are shared in the app once you're set up.

What counts as an event?

Any World Cup market you trade on Kalshi — match outcomes, advancement, group winners, the tournament winner, and more. Ten or more distinct trades across the tournament keeps you active.

Do I have to bet a certain way?

No. Trade your own way — there are no right or wrong picks. We're measuring real outcomes, not coaching you.

Why prediction markets?

We're testing whether everyday bettors actually do better trading prediction markets than at their sportsbook. Your sportsbook history is the baseline; your Kalshi trades are the comparison.
