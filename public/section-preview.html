Why Fade

We all bet.
We just don’t bet together.

Your picks live on one app. Your friends’ picks live in the group chat. The cappers you follow live on X. And every “lock of the year” lives in a screenshot you’re squinting at, trying to punch into your sportsbook before the line moves.

Betting’s always been social — the apps just never were.

Until now. Fade brings the whole betting community into one app — track picks in real time, get notified when someone places a bet, break down your history and your fantasy team with your own AI assistant, and export straight to your sportsbook. One app for everything betting.
