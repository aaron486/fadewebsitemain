Common Questions

Is my sportsbook login safe?
Yes. FadeSync uses encrypted, read-only connections. We never store your sportsbook passwords, and we can't place bets or make changes to your account.
Which bet types are supported?
Straight bets, parlays, same-game parlays, teasers, round robins, futures, and props. If your sportsbook tracks it, Fade syncs it.
How fast do bets sync?
Most bets appear on your Fade profile within minutes of being placed. Live bets and in-play wagers sync as they settle.
Can I connect multiple sportsbooks?
Absolutely. Link as many books as you want. Your Fade profile aggregates everything into one unified record across all your accounts.
What if my sportsbook isn't listed?
We're adding new integrations regularly. bet365, Hard Rock, BetRivers, and more are on the roadmap. Request a book through the app and we'll prioritize it.
Does Fade work with daily fantasy or casino bets?
Fade is focused on sports betting and prediction markets. DFS and casino integrations are not currently supported.
