Step 1 · Make sure you qualify

Do you qualify?

The study recruits active U.S. sportsbook bettors who are new to prediction markets. Quick check:

  • ✓You’re 21+ and bet in the U.S.
  • ✓You have an active sportsbook account with real history — roughly 90 days and 50+ bets.
  • ✓You’re new to prediction markets — 5 or fewer prior Kalshi-style trades.
  • ✓You’re willing to trade on Kalshi for the 8-week window.
Not a perfect match? You can still download Fade and follow along with the community group.
📱
Step 2 · Get set up

Download Fade & sign up.

Fade is where you’ll join the cohort and connect your books.

  • 1Install Fade from the App Store or Google Play.
  • 2Create your account and verify your email or phone.
  • 3Confirm you’re 21+ and tap Join the Study.
Tip: Make sure it’s the official app — the publisher should be Fade Social LLC.
🔗
Step 3 · Connect with Fade Sync

Sync your sportsbooks.

Fade Sync securely links the books you already use and imports your betting history — read-only, no money moves. This becomes your personal baseline.

  • 1Tap Connect a Sportsbook and choose your book.
  • 2Securely sign in to authorize a read-only connection.
  • 3Connect every book you use so your baseline is complete.
Read-only by design. Fade Sync can see your bets and outcomes to measure performance. It can never deposit, withdraw, or place wagers.
🛡
Step 4 · Consent & privacy

What you’re agreeing to.

Participation is voluntary and uses your own money. Before you start, here’s the deal:

  • ✓You trade with your own capital — Fade never places bets for you.
  • ✓Your data is anonymized in any published results.
  • ✓The study’s funder is disclosed to every participant.
  • ✓You can leave the study at any time.
This is research — not financial or betting advice. Trading involves risk of loss.
📅
Step 5 · How it runs

What happens next.

Two phases, then we publish. Here’s the path:

  • 1Baseline. We establish your sportsbook history (≈90 days, 50+ bets).
  • 2Treatment. 8 weeks trading sports on Kalshi — aim for 30+ trades, with 80%+ of your volume on Kalshi.
  • 3Publish. Anonymized results and analysis released openly at week 12.
Trade your own way — there are no right or wrong picks. We’re measuring outcomes, not coaching.
