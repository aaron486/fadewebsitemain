Nate Robinson × Fade

Concept: "Can't Fade Nate." Hero spot + social cutdowns to roll from the World Cup through the 2026 College Football season.

Shoot Date
Friday, June 20, 2026
Locations
Trill Burgers → Barbershop → Court
Concept

Can't Fade Nate.

One word, three meanings. Nate sits in the barber's chair getting a fade — while the story plays on how the world has tried to fade him (bet against him) his whole career. Too short. Overlooked. Doubted. And still a 3× NBA Slam Dunk champion. The punchline: you can't fade Nate — and on Fade, every underdog gets their shot.

Brand voice: confident, social, fun. Fade is the pre-bet / social layer — not a sportsbook.

Shoot Plan

Three stops, one story.

Set mock-ups below are AI-generated concept frames (stand-in talent) for reference only.

Stop 1 · Run-and-gun

Trill Burgers Appearance

Start at Nate's appearance at Trill Burgers. Capture B-roll — Nate interacting with fans, signing, crowd energy, candid moments. Documentary feel; grab the real reactions.

Stop 2 · Controlled

The Barbershop

Move to the barbershop for the hero. Clean, lit shots of Nate getting his cut while he delivers the lines. This is the spine of the spot.

Stop 3 · Energy

Basketball Court

Close on the court — Nate dribbling, shooting, laughing. Doing what he does best. The visual payoff to "too short to last."
