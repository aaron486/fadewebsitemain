The social layer for sports betting.

Fade brings the whole betting world into one app — your friends, the biggest names in sports, and the sharpest cappers — every bet they place, the moment they place it.

The Thesis

Be where people go before they bet.

Every bet starts with a decision — and that decision is made before a sportsbook is ever opened. Today it happens scattered across group chats, X, stat sites and screenshots.

Fade is building the single place people go before they bet. We don't have to be the book. If we own the pre-bet moment, we own the most valuable real estate in betting — and we win on advertising and affiliate, not on risk.

The Product

Four reasons people open Fade before they bet.

Social, narratives, betting tools, and media — the four pulls that make Fade the pre-bet destination, and the surface where we sell advertising and affiliate.

01 · Social

Your friends' picks, instantly

You get a notification the moment a friend makes a pick. Betting is social — but sharing bets has always meant screenshots and group chats. Fade solves that on day one.

02 · Narratives & Streaks

The story behind the bet

Fade brings the qualitative side of betting into the app — who's on what, why a player's bad on the road, celebrity bets, player quirks and streaks. The stuff sports fans actually care about lives here.

03 · Betting Assistant

Real tools & stats

The tools and numbers people actually use — casual research, your full betting history, and Fade IQ, the AI assistant that breaks it all down.

04 · Media & Culture

Partnerships, original media, and culture — reaching the largest community of sports bettors. This is where Fade dominates and separates.

Competitive Landscape

Trackers proved the demand. Fade owns the moment.

A wave of bet-tracking apps has shown that bettors want to sync their books and see real numbers — but they all live after the bet is placed. Fade is built around the moment before it.

Competitor

Pikkit

Bet tracker that auto-syncs 30+ sportsbooks (read-only). Free core + Pikkit Pro; ~$9M raised, $10B+ in tracked bets. Strong analytics — light on social and content.

Competitor

Juice Reel

Bet tracker + picks marketplace syncing 300+ books/DFS; 250k+ bettors, buy/sell picks and AI predictions. A marketplace layer — not a media or social destination.

Fade

The pre-bet destination

Media + social + analytics + narratives in one app — where people go before they bet. We monetize attention through advertising & affiliate, not a tracker subscription.
