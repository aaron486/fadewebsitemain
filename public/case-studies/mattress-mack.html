He Turned a Mattress Store Into the Best Bet in Sports History

How Houston's “Mattress Mack” turned furniture promotions into the most famous hedge in sports betting — and a bet he literally cannot lose.

Fade Desk · 13 slides

Jim “Mattress Mack” McIngvale built Gallery Furniture into a $200M Houston institution on relentless, larger-than-life marketing. But his most famous move was never about furniture — it was about turning the Astros into the best advertising money can buy.

By promising customers their money back if the home team wins, then hedging with millions in real sports bets, Mack engineered a promotion he literally cannot lose: if the Astros win, his betting winnings cover the refunds; if they lose, he keeps all the furniture revenue. This is how a mattress salesman became the most famous bettor in sports.
